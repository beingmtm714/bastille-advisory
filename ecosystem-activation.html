How It Works
Before launch
Event Setup

Participants are seeded into your chosen format — brackets, demo days, hackathons. Scale and structure adapt to your roster and goals.

Ongoing
Onchain Voting

Community members buy votes in your native token to back their favorites. Cost increases over time, rewarding early conviction over bandwagon support.

Ongoing
Rewards Pool

All voter spend flows into a shared pool. Backers of winning teams earn a proportionate return — every vote is a transaction on your chain.

Ongoing
Daily Momentum

New matchups run Mon–Fri for the full campaign. Each round generates a fresh content cycle, compounding narrative and transaction volume through every round.

For Your Ecosystem
Your whole ecosystem markets itself simultaneously — every project campaigns, every community member picks sides. The result is sustained onchain activity and narrative without running separate campaigns for each project.
For Your Projects
Every project gets a real competitive moment in front of the whole ecosystem's audience, not just their own followers. Bigger projects bring crowds that discover smaller ones — the rising tide lifts every team.
For Your Community
For the first time, backing a project means having genuine skin in the game — not just a retweet. Community members earn real returns when they back winners, which means they actually care who wins.
Case Study
Avalanche × Confetti 2025

Bastille designed and ran this campaign end-to-end for Avalanche on Confetti's onchain incentive platform — 24 ecosystem teams competing daily for 30 rounds. Projects competed for mindshare, voters competed for returns, and every round drove a new transaction cycle through the platform. It became the highest-revenue engagement in Confetti's history and established a replicable playbook for any ecosystem.
