How It Works
Before launch
Event Setup

Participants are seeded and matched across the field. Structure and scale adapt to the size of your roster and the length of the campaign.

Ongoing
Onchain Voting

Community members buy votes to take a position on who wins. Cost increases over time, so backing the right team early earns the greatest return.

Ongoing
Voter Rewards

Voter funds flow into a pool paid out to backers of winning teams. Every vote costs real money — which rewards conviction and makes bot manipulation self-defeating.

Ongoing
Daily Momentum

New matchups run through the full campaign — each round gives projects a new moment to campaign and the community a fresh reason to show up.
