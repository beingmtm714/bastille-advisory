What I Found

The diagnostic from our first call named four gaps that resolve into one commercial function:

  • You "don't want a repeat of the last raise."

So this proposal is the down-scope: the two pieces that have to move before September, in order. Raise readiness leads, because the clock is shortest there. Commercial architecture follows, scoped and ready to build. Both are below.
