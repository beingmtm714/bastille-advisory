How It Works
The Mechanic

Head-to-Head. Every Day.

Projects are seeded into a bracket. Each matchup runs 24–48 hrs on JokeRace — community members buy votes with native tokens, and voters who backed the winner earn a share of the rewards pool. Every round drives a new transaction cycle.

Why It Compounds

Every Team Becomes a Marketer

Because votes cost real money and winners earn real rewards, every participating project has a genuine reason to mobilize their community. You get 24 teams running their own parallel campaigns — all pointing back to your ecosystem.

Tournament Structure

Four Rounds. One Champion.

01
Bracket Setup

16–32 projects seeded into single-elimination. Avalanche ran 24. Each matchup launches at a set time with a price curve that rewards early voters.

02
Onchain Voting

Community buys votes with native tokens. Cost increases over time — early conviction is rewarded, late voters pay a premium.

03
Rewards Pool

All voter spend flows into a pool. Winners' backers earn a proportionate share — paid automatically onchain at round close.

04
Daily Momentum

New matchups Mon–Fri. Teams rally, ecosystem posts recaps, and narrative momentum compounds across every successive round.

Case Study
Avalanche × Confetti (JokeRace) 2025

Bastille designed and ran this campaign end-to-end for Avalanche on Confetti's onchain incentive platform — 24 ecosystem teams competing daily for 30 rounds. Projects competed for mindshare, voters competed for returns, and every round drove a new transaction cycle through the platform. It became the highest-revenue engagement in Confetti's history and established a replicable playbook for any ecosystem.
