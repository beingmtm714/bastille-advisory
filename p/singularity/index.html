Singularity:
Retention & Acquisition Framework

For Ahyaan Sayed, Naman, Ujwal

Executive Summary

This engagement builds the systems required to retain Singularity's existing community, convert its protocol pipeline into onboarded users, and prepare the ground for its next fundraising conversation. Month 1 delivers a community engagement and advisory board framework, a configured outreach system handed off ready for use, and consolidated documentation for internal reference. Expected outcomes are a functioning retention cadence, an outreach system primed for BD execution, and baseline metrics establishing a measurement point for Month 2 results. Month 2, scoped at Month 1 close-out, extends this work into live operation, fundraising preparation, and advisory support for the compute futures product as it develops.

Current Status

  • 14 protocols in active conversation; no onboarding path currently exists for them.
  • 40 members in the Telegram community; no fixed communication cadence in place.
  • Testnet launch timeline not yet finalized internally.
  • Prior funding conversation (a16z) concluded pending demonstrated user and developer traction.

Scope of Work

  • Community
    Retention

    Builds the system that keeps the current community engaged and channels its highest-leverage members into a structured advisory board.

    • Engagement and retention playbook: communication cadence, content themes, response protocols, and new-protocol entry sequence.
    • Tooling configuration (content calendar scheduling) plus baseline engagement metrics.
    • Advisory board of the community's highest-leverage members (not simply the most active): product feedback, testnet validation, and a referral channel into Acquisition.
      • Scoring criteria weighted toward leverage and expertise, not activity.
      • Onboarding sequence: expectations, cadence, compensation.
      • Documented rotation and offboarding process.
    • Feedback-capture mechanism routing input into product and roadmap decisions.
  • Acquisition

    Turns the existing protocol database into an outreach system BD associates can run directly, with a defined path for what happens once a protocol converts.

    • Review of current outreach tooling, with recommendations where gaps exist.
    • Outreach tooling selected and configured: sequencing, follow-up timing, deliverability safeguards.
    • Tooling handed off primed with the leads list (including advisory-board referrals) and baseline conversion metrics.
    • Conversion process defining next steps once a protocol confirms interest, handing off into the community entry sequence.
  • Playbook
    Documentation

    Consolidates the retention and acquisition systems into documentation the team can run without ongoing involvement from Bastille.

    • Written system covering the retention framework and outreach tooling configuration.
    • Structured for internal use, without ongoing dependency on this engagement.
  • General
    Advisory

    Provides advisory availability across organizational, positioning, and fundraising topics beyond the deliverables defined above.

    • Advisory calls available as needed (~8 hours), beyond scheduled check-ins.
    • Coverage: organizational strategy, positioning, investor and fundraising preparation.
    • GTM strategy addressed directly through Acquisition and Community Retention above.
    • Advisory support for compute futures (or successor product) discovery, alongside the workstreams above.
